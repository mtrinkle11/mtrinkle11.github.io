ABOUT ME

Committed to success

My German heritage taught me a strong work ethic, that any job worth doing is worth doing right the first time, and to always leave things better than when you found them. Growing up in the US taught me to only accept success, that hard work pays off, and that a man is only as good as his word. Living in Mexico for 12 years taught me that I should not be so individualistic, that together we can do more, and that those around me become my family and family is first whether at work or at home. For twenty years I served with all I have to leave this world a better place than I found it, as a youth minister, community developer, and teacher. I am happy to have helped many change their lives for the better.
